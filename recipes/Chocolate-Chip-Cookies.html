Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Crispy surface but soft and chewy inides which will surely bless your tastebuds.

This recipe should take around an hour to make

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

STEPS

    • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    • Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
    • Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
    • Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
    • Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
    • Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
